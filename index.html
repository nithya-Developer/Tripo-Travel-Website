About Tripo

Tripo is a Vacation rental company. The idea behind Tripo is simple: Find a way for local people to make some extra money renting out their spare home or room to people visiting the area.
Hosts using this platform get to advertise their rentals to millions of people worldwide, with the reassurance that a big company will handle payments and offer support when needed.
And for guests, Tripo can offer a homey place to stay that has more character, perhaps even with a kitchen to avoid dining out, often at a lower price than what hotels charge.
